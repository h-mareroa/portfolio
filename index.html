Official Limited Use
Publication, June 2021
210mm x 297mm
This publication was an exploration of sequencial imagery and text across twelve spreads. I was randomly assigned three categories to develop/discover content within, and choose to focus on infamous cults across the United States. The archival style of content gave me the chance to play with the level of black on the page and scan multiple times to distort how information is read.
The Irishman
Video, June 2021
1920 x 1080
Title sequence made for the crime based film, The Irishman. My title sequence is based on a previous Saul Bass title sequence made for another Martin Scorsese movie, Goodfellas. This title sequence focused on a simple use of texture, colour and shapes in the same style as Saul Bass.
Blackhole
Interactive website, Nov 2021
blackhole.stuffmov.es
An interactive website that was based on the artwork of Emily Karaka. Her work includes an expressive use of colour and texture, which was the focus for this website. The still background images were made with milk and dye, and the squares all move via CSS animation.
